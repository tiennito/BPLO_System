Re-Newal Application

To renew a business permit, please ensure that the following requirements are updated and passed again.

  • Business Permit CopyUpdated and valid copy of the current permit.
  • Barangay ClearanceClearance issued within the last 6 months.
  • DTI/SEC RegistrationUpdated DTI or SEC Certificate.
  • Valid IDGovernment-issued ID of the owner/authorized representative.
  • Other Required DocumentsAny additional documents required by BPLO.

Please ensure all documents are complete and legible to avoid delays in processing.
